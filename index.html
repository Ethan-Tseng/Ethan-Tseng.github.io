Ethan Tseng

About

I am a CS PhD student at Princeton University advised by Felix Heide in the Princeton Computational Imaging Lab. My research interests are in computational imaging, computer vision, and computer graphics. I am currently researching end-to-end optimization methods for developing specialized optics and imaging pipelines. I received my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University.
